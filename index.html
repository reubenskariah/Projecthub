How ProjectHub Works

1. Post a Project Call

Enter your project details, creator contact info, and select from over 500+ tech tags. Set a review window (1 to 14 days) and guide details.

2. Organizer Review

New project calls enter the review queue. Once approved by an organizer in the separate admin portal, they are pushed live to the top of the feed.

3. Form Your Team

Interested students apply with their LinkedIn profile. Confirmed applications fill slots immediately; subsequent applicants join the waitlist.
